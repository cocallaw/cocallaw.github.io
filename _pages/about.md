Corey works as a Senior Cloud Solution Architect (CSA) on the US National CSA Team at Microsoft, helping incubate different technology scenarios and enable customer adoption in the infrastructure space. Prior to joining the US National Team, Corey spent working with a Microsoft Partner as a consultant covering Azure and Office 365, and as a Support Engineer for Exchange Server.

With a primary focus on Azure Infrastructure topics, Corey shares tips, tricks and lessons learned from working with Azure and a wide range of customers during day job. He enjoys finding new and creative ways to solve problems and automate solutions, you can find many of his creations in his [GitHub](https://github.com/cocallaw) with some of his favorites being documented on this blog.
